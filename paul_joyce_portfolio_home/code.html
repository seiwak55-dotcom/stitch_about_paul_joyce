About Me

Composer, Producer & Orchestrator

Paul Joyce is a composer, producer, and orchestrator specializing in film scores, documentaries, and audiovisual projects. His music combines orchestral writing, contemporary textures, and electronic elements, exploring a sonic language that seeks emotional depth and narrative expression through sound.

He studied at the Fundació Conservatori Superior del Liceu and previously at the Taller de Músics. His autodidactic journey began at age 13, when he discovered his passion for film music.
