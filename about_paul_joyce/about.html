Paul Joyce is a composer, producer, and orchestrator specializing in film scores, documentaries, and audiovisual projects. His music combines orchestral writing, contemporary textures, and electronic elements, exploring a sonic language that seeks emotional depth and narrative expression through sound.

He studied at the Fundació Conservatori Superior del Liceu with renowned composers such as Montserrat Torras, Benet Casablancas, Ramón Humet, and Octavi Rumbau, and previously at the Taller de Músics. He complements his solid academic foundation with a strong autodidactic path that began at the age of 13, when he discovered his passion for film music.

He has composed original scores for short films and documentaries, including Pintura i Cadenes, and has collaborated on multidisciplinary projects combining music and visual arts. His concert works have been performed at the Museu Picasso in Barcelona and the Conservatori Superior del Liceu, and he was a finalist in the II Composition Competition of the OSVCV.
